About Us

welcome

Hello and welcome to How To Pasta. We are a small but mighty group of enthusiastic lovers of pasta and would like spread that joy to the people of London by teaching you all how to make fresh pasta.


We provide a public and private venue for our cooking classes with a fully equipped kitchen so you will not need to bring anything with you come. Prepare to become a pro in all things pasta.

In the class

In the first half of the class we go through the step by step process of making pasta dough by with the use of fresh eggs and flour. Because we be making different shapes we will making multiple sets of dough.


In the second half of our class we will begin making some basic shapes with our dough that we made in the first half of the class. we will make Gnocchi, Garganelli, Pappardelle and Ravioli. You will also be introduced some pasta making tools to help make these shapes.

Why learn to make pasta

It's Healthier

Making pasta with fresh ingredients provides better nutritional value and easier for your bodies digestion.

Quick to make

Making pasta does not taking that much time or effort to make. It can be done without a pasta machine and done entirely by hand and can be made into any shape you want.

It Tastes better

Fresh pasta tastes far better than store bought pasta. Because of the use of eggs in the dough it gives the pasta a fresh taste.

It costs less

It costs more for you to buy pasta than it is to make it. The only ingredents you need are flour, eggs and salt. All things you already have in your home.
