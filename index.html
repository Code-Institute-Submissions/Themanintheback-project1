Why learn to make pasta

It's Healthier

Making pasta with fresh ingredients provides better nutritional value and easier for your bodies digestion.

Quick to make

Making pasta does not taking that much time or effort to make. It can be done without a pasta machine and done entirely by hand and can be made into any shape you want.

It Tastes better

Fresh pasta tastes far better than store bought pasta. Because of the use of eggs in the dough it gives the pasta a fresh taste.

It costs less

It costs more for you to buy pasta than it is to make it. The only ingredents you need are flour, eggs and salt. All things you already have in your home.
