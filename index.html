Why learn to make pasta

It's Healthier

Making pasta with fresh ingredients provides better nutritional value and easier for your bodies digestion.

Do's not Take time to make

Making pasta do's not taking that much time or effort to make. It can be done without a pasta machine and done entirely by hand and can made into any shape you want.

It's Taste's better

Fresh pasta taste's far better than store bought pasta. Because of the use eggs used the dough it gives the pasta a fresh taste.

It's more cost effective

It's costs more for you to buy pasta than it is to make it. The only ingredents you need are flour, eggs and salt. All things you already have in your home.
